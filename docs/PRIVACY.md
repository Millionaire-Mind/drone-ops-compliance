## What data is processed

When you use this service, it may process:

* Location (latitude/longitude)
* Planned time of flight
* Planned altitude
* Mission type (e.g., recreational vs. Part 107 commercial)

## How data is used

Data is used only to:

* Retrieve airspace/authorization context (via configured providers)
* Retrieve weather conditions (e.g., METAR)
* Retrieve temporary flight restrictions (TFRs)
* Generate an advisory checklist and recommendation

## Data storage

By default, this service is designed not to store precise location/time history.
Operational logs may include anonymized request identifiers and error metrics for reliability.

## Third-party services

To provide results, the service may call third-party data sources (e.g., aviation weather and airspace providers). Information you provide may be transmitted to those services as part of the lookup.

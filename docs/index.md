\# Drone Ops \& Compliance



This service provides \*\*read-only, advisory drone pre-flight checks\*\* for operations in the United States.



It is designed to be used through ChatGPT via OpenAIâ€™s App Store using GPT Actions.



---



\## What this service does



It provides:



\- Airspace classification and authorization indicators  

\- Weather conditions and Part 107-style advisory checks  

\- FAA Temporary Flight Restriction (TFR) lookups  

\- A consolidated \*\*GO / GO-WITH-CONDITIONS / NO-GO\*\* style preflight checklist  

\- Links to FAA-approved LAANC providers (no submissions are made)



---



\## What this service does NOT do



This service:



\- Does \*\*not\*\* submit LAANC authorizations  

\- Does \*\*not\*\* provide legal advice  

\- Does \*\*not\*\* grant permission to fly  

\- Does \*\*not\*\* replace FAA-approved tools



All outputs are \*\*advisory only\*\*.
